Best Ways to Remove Turmeric Stains from Clothes

Turmeric is a staple in Indian households, but its bright yellow color can leave stubborn stains on clothes. Here are simple home remedies to remove turmeric stains using lemon, baking soda, vinegar and more.

Easy Methods to Remove Turmeric Stains

Lemon Juice & Salt

Mix lemon juice and salt to form a paste. Apply on stain for 30 minutes then rinse with cold water.

Baking Soda Paste

Mix baking soda with water to make paste. Apply on stain and leave for 30 minutes.

Vinegar Soak

Soak the stained cloth in vinegar and water solution for 2-3 hours before washing.

Hydrogen Peroxide

Apply directly to stain for 10-15 minutes then rinse. Works like natural bleach.

Dish Soap

Dish soap breaks down turmeric oil making stains easier to remove.

Importance of Acting Fast

When it comes to stains, time matters. The faster you treat a stain, the easier it is to remove. Waiting allows stains to bond with fabric fibers.

Step-by-Step Stain Removal

  1. Act fast and rinse stain immediately.
  2. Use cold water from the back side of fabric.
  3. Blot with cloth (do not rub).
  4. Repeat rinsing until stain fades.

Tools You Need for Stain Removal

  • Cold Water
  • Clean Cloths
  • Baking Soda
  • White Vinegar
  • Mild Detergent
  • Spray Bottle
