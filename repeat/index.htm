Get started with Rinse Repeat

If it fits in the bag, we take care of it.

Rinse Repeat plans are priced by the bag, not the pound. Select the number of bags you need each month and enjoy service 7 days a week.

Returned fresh and ready to wear.

Your clothes get their own machine, are separated by lights and darks, cleaned according to your preferences, and delivered neatly folded to your door – we even pair your socks.

Meet your Rinse Repeat bag

Your Rinse Repeat bag is custom designed with you in mind.

Each bag fits two large laundry baskets worth of clothes (see sizing below). The bag has two handles on top making it easy (and safer!) to carry for you and your Rinse Valet. Its sleek design and zipper top make it useful for laundry, and we have also seen customers using it at the gym and for travel.

Your Valet will bring you your very own Rinse Repeat bag on your first pickup.
